Web page backgrounds used to be commonplace, but they became unpopular once designers figured out that visitors to web pages didn’t want their eyes wrenched out by gaudy tiled background patterns. With text being as hard to read on-screen as it is, it’s adding insult to injury to inflict some nasty paisley mosaic background (or worse) on the poor reader, too.
